Agentic Chargeback Automation for BFSI

FSS — Financial Services Sector

LangGraphMCP ServersFastAPILangSmith

Challenge

Solution

Results

  • ✓Replaced 6-person manual ops team
  • ✓Full LangSmith & Langfuse observability
  • ✓Zero compliance escalations since go-live

LLM Fine-Tuning & Inference Optimization

Enterprise AI — Production Inference

vLLMLoRAGPTQNVIDIA GPUs

Challenge

High inference costs and slow throughput on fine-tuned LLMs serving production traffic at scale.

Solution

Fine-tuned domain-specific LLMs (Qwen, DeepSeek) using LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT on PyTorch with Hugging Face on AWS SageMaker. Applied GPTQ and AWQ quantization. Deployed vLLM-based inference on mixed GPU/CPU infrastructure with optimized batching and KV cache tuning.

Results

  • ✓3x throughput improvement
  • ✓35% inference cost reduction
  • ✓40% smaller model footprint
  • ✓No accuracy degradation
