The Story

Empyron Solutions started as an independent AI consultancy in Koblenz, Germany — delivering end-to-end ML and GenAI solutions for SMEs across logistics, retail, and SaaS. What began as solo consulting grew into a full-stack AI engineering practice.

With an M.Sc. in Web and Data Science and 5 years of hands-on experience — from building scalable ETL pipelines at MEBEDO GmbH to architecting production multi-agent systems at Expleo — we bring deep technical rigor to every engagement.

Our stack spans the full modern AI landscape: LangGraph agents, RAG/GraphRAG pipelines, LoRA/QLoRA fine-tuning on Qwen and DeepSeek, vLLM inference on NVIDIA GPUs, and full LLM observability with LangSmith and Langfuse.
