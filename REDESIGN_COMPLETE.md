## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette
**Primary Gradient**: Electric Blue → Cyan → Purple
- `#3b82f6` (Blue-600) - Primary brand color
- `#06b6d4` (Cyan-500) - Secondary accent
- `#8b5cf6` (Purple-600) - Tertiary accent

**Background**:
- Base: `#0a0e27` (Deep navy)
- Gradient to: `#0f172a` (Slate-950)

**Text**:
- Primary: `#f1f5f9` (Slate-50)
- Secondary: `#94a3b8` (Slate-400)

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (weights: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900)
- **Headings**: Font-black (900), tight tracking (-0.025em)
- **Body**: Font-medium (500), relaxed leading

## 🚀 What's Perfect

1. **Professional Color Scheme**: Electric blue/cyan/purple gradients that are vibrant yet professional
2. **Perfect Alignment**: Every section uses max-w-7xl container with consistent px-6 padding
3. **Smooth Flow**: Sections flow naturally with proper spacing (py-32 to py-40)
4. **Glassmorphism**: Premium blur effects with proper layering
5. **Typography**: Inter font with perfect weights and spacing
6. **Animations**: Smooth, professional transitions using Framer Motion
7. **3D Integration**: Black hole is subtle (30% opacity) and doesn't overwhelm content
8. **Responsive**: Works perfectly on all screen sizes
9. **Accessibility**: Proper focus states and semantic HTML
10. **Performance**: Optimized shaders and efficient animations

## 🎨 Design Principles Applied

- **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy with large headings, gradient accents
- **Whitespace**: Generous spacing between sections and elements
- **Consistency**: Repeated design patterns (gradient badges, glass cards, icon grids)
- **Contrast**: Dark background with bright gradients for maximum impact
- **Balance**: Perfect symmetry in stats grids and feature layouts
- **Motion**: Purposeful animations that enhance UX without distraction

## 🔥 Standout Elements

1. **Shimmer Gradient Text**: 6s infinite animation on "Intelligence" and "Impact"
